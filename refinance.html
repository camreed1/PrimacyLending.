Refinancing Questions

Common questions about refinancing your home

When does it make sense to refinance?

Generally, if you can lower your rate by 0.5% or more, or if you need to access equity for important expenses. We'll help you calculate if refinancing makes financial sense.

How much equity do I need for a cash-out refinance?

Most lenders require you to maintain at least 20% equity after the cash-out, meaning you can typically access up to 80% of your home's value.

What are the costs involved in refinancing?

Closing costs typically range from 2-5% of the loan amount, including appraisal, title insurance, and lender fees. We'll provide a detailed breakdown upfront.

How long does the refinance process take?

Most refinances close within 30-45 days from application. Streamline refinances for government loans can often close faster.

Can I refinance if I have an FHA or VA loan?

Yes! FHA and VA loans have streamline refinance programs that often require minimal documentation and no appraisal, making the process faster and easier.
