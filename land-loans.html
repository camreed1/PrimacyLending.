Land Loan Questions

Common questions about land financing

What's the difference between a land loan and a mortgage?

Land loans are specifically for purchasing vacant land, while mortgages are for homes with existing structures. Land loans typically have higher down payment requirements and shorter terms.

How much down payment do I need for a land loan?

Down payment requirements vary based on the land type and intended use, typically ranging from 10% to 50%. We'll help you understand the requirements for your specific situation.

Can I get a land loan if I plan to build later?

Yes! We can structure your land loan to potentially convert to a construction loan when you're ready to build, streamlining the process.

What factors affect land loan approval?

Key factors include your credit score, income, debt-to-income ratio, the land's location, accessibility, and intended use. We'll review all these factors during your consultation.

How long are land loan terms?

Land loan terms are typically shorter than traditional mortgages, often ranging from 5 to 20 years depending on the loan type and lender requirements.
