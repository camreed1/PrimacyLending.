Service Requirements

WWII, Korea, Vietnam: 90 days active duty
Gulf War (after 9/7/80): 24 months or full period called to active duty
Peacetime: 181 days continuous active duty
Post-9/11: 90 days active duty

VA Loan Questions

Common questions about VA loans answered

What is the VA funding fee and can it be waived?

The VA funding fee ranges from 1.25% to 3.3% of the loan amount and can be rolled into the loan. It's waived for veterans with service-connected disabilities and surviving spouses.

Can I use my VA loan benefit more than once?

Yes, your VA loan benefit is reusable. You can use it again after paying off your previous VA loan or by having sufficient remaining entitlement.

Are there property restrictions with VA loans?

The property must be your primary residence and meet VA's Minimum Property Requirements (MPRs) for safety, sanitation, and structural soundness.

What is the maximum VA loan amount?

There's no maximum loan amount, but the VA guarantees up to 25% of the loan. In most counties, you can borrow up to $766,550 with no down payment.

Can I buy a multi-unit property with a VA loan?

Yes, you can buy up to a 4-unit property as long as you occupy one unit as your primary residence.
